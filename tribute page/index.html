About

Homi Jehangir Bhabha (1909–1966) was an Indian nuclear physicist, founding director of the Tata Institute of Fundamental Research (TIFR) and the key architect of India’s nuclear energy program.

Timeline

  • 1909: Born in Mumbai
  • 1945: Founded TIFR
  • 1948: Chairman, Atomic Energy Commission of India
  • 1966: Passed away in a plane crash

Major Contributions

Nuclear Physics

Developed Bhabha scattering theory.

Institutions

Founded TIFR and supported scientific research in India.

Nuclear Energy

Laid the foundation of India’s nuclear power program.

Famous Quote

"No power is more costly than ignorance."
